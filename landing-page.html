About स्वास्थ्यmitra

स्वास्थ्यmitra is a leading healthcare provider in Nepal. With a commitment to excellence, we provide a wide range of medical services to meet the needs of our patients.A Nepal-focused healthcare platform built with modern technology to connect patients with qualified healthcare professionals across the country. Through advanced medical technologies, cutting-edge treatments, and a patient-centered approach, we strive every day to elevate the health and well-being of those we serve. Above all, we ensure that each patient feels respected, seen, and cared for with dignity.We commit to improving lives through state-of-the-art technologies, evidence-based innovation, and a compassionate team of professionals—serving all, regardless of background or circumstance. With advanced facilities and a multidisciplinary team, we aim to raise the standard of care—ensuring each patient's journey is safe, respectful, and results-driven.
